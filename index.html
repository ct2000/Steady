About STEADY

Built for calmer responses. · Gold Release

STEADY Pro is a practical self-help app designed to slow reactions, reduce emotional escalation, and support clearer choices during difficult moments.

BuildSTEADY Pro Version 1.0
Developer / OwnerLee Whittaker
FocusRegulation · CBT · Communication

When to step away

  • You feel close to shouting, threatening, breaking things, or violence.
  • You are under the influence of alcohol or drugs.
  • The other person is escalating or unsafe.
  • You cannot think clearly enough to be fair.

UK urgent support

If there is immediate danger, call 999.

If you feel at risk of harming yourself or someone else, contact urgent local support, NHS 111, your GP, or a crisis line.

This toolkit is self-help, not a substitute for professional care.

App philosophy

The goal is not to suppress anger — it’s to respond more clearly, calmly, and intentionally.
